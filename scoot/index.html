ABOUT THE GAME

SCOOT is a scooter trick game where you can pull off sick tricks, grind rails, and explore indoor and outdoor parks.


🛴 Tricks: Tail whip, front flip, back flip, 180, 360 and more spins, bar spin, bri, slap the finger, and no-handing.


🏟️ Locations: Ride outdoors with trees, plants, birds, and wind — or head inside to the indoor park with grind rails, boxes, drop-ins, and sick lighting.


🛒 Shop: Earn coins doing tricks and spend them on new scooters and grip tape in the shop.


Designed by a future pro scooter rider 🔥
